Emily Vandenbroeck is an aspiring writer, currently in her third year of her bachelor's degree in Creative Writing at RMIT University. Emily's interests lie in writing fiction novels, short- and long-form creative non-fiction, and autofiction. She draws inspiration from life experiences, and observations made in her day-to-day life.

Emily had wanted to be an author in childhood, but then an illustrator, a gymnast, an equestrian, a veterinarian and a librarian. She took her time, but eventually, returned to her roots. You can read more from her via the Substack linked below.

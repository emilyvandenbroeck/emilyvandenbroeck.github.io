In November of 2024, I was lucky enough to take part in the Creative Acts as Civil Action program with RMIT, photo.circle, and Pathshala Institute, as a New Columbo Plan recipient. Being a creative writing student in a program heavy on visual arts, I was challenged to broaden the ways in which I write, and how I can apply my skills within multidisciplinary projects.

I took part in two projects with my peers, assisting in collating information and knowledge, utilising my quick typing for transcription of audio and video files, and eventually, my writing ability to make translations more coherent, and write any additional content for the website.

Below, you will find a link to the Sikti Forest Plant Archive, and a video titled Sikti Forest Ambience, which takes the viewer on a walk through the Sikti Forest.
